Create Collage

Select 2-4 photos from above to create a collage

How to use:

  1. Click "Start Camera" to enable your camera
  2. Select camera and mode (front/back/ultra-wide)
  3. Use "Invert Camera" to flip the image if needed
  4. Click "Take Photo" to capture images
  5. Click the eye icon to preview any photo
  6. Select 2-4 photos from the gallery
  7. Choose a collage layout
  8. Download your collage!
